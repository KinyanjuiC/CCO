About the Project

The Cloud Computing Optimization project aims to bridge the gap in cloud service accessibility for underprivileged students in Sub-Saharan Africa. By focusing on the safety, scalability, and performance of cloud services, CCO empowers students with essential academic resources that support their growth and learning.
